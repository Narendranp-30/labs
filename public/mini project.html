What is Agro Yields?

Agro Yields Agro Products Pvt Ltd, founded in sep 2022, is a fresh dairy products company based in perundurai, Erode, India. AY's prime motive is to establish direct trade between local farmers and consumers. AY provides products including farm-fresh Milk, Ghee, Honey and Palkova. Today AY is one of Tamilnadu's leading brands, merchandising fresh dairy products.
